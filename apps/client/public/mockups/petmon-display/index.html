PETMON Display (TV) — Mockups

The agreed direction from docs/petmon-design.md rendered as throwaway HTML: a DMG-green Game Boy screen letterboxed inside the broadcast stage. Sprites here are procedurally dithered placeholders — real ones come from the generator.

Open full-screen on a 16:9 window for a TV-accurate preview. Both pages self-animate so you can judge the text-box rhythm (typewriter + ~1.6s auto-advance) without a host driving them.
