ROUND_RESULTS — Mockups

Three takes on the end-of-round summary. Each interprets "round complete" differently — drama, information, or quiet finality.

01

Round Slam

Three big stacked numbers (Wing / Mini-Game / Round Total) with animated reveal. Consistent with the beat-reveal language. Aggregate across all teams.

02

Per-Team Breakdown

Grid showing each team's round earnings (wing + game + total). Players see exactly how their team did this round. Most informative — but requires per-team prop wiring.

03

Banner Lockup

Banner-style "ROUND 1 LOCKED" headline with small breakdown chips below. Quiet "scores deposited" feel. Leans on the standings footer to do the showing.
