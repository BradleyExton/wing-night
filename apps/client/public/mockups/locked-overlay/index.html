GameLockedOverlay — Mockups

Three takes on the pre-round-1 lock overlay. Each mockup simulates the overlay sitting on top of the Cinematic Inferno SETUP body (which shows through faintly). Active countdown state shown.

01

Beat Reveal

Same staggered cadence as the intros — "Stand by" → "Locked In" → countdown. Most thematically consistent with the rest of the design language.

02

Countdown Spotlight

Massive countdown digit fills the viewport. Tiny "Round 1" eyebrow. Most focused / suspenseful — the count IS the experience.

03

Heat Locked

Gold lock icon with rotating glow ring, "Locked In" headline, countdown circled by an animated heat ring. Most theatrical and game-show-y.
