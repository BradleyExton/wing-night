Trivia Display — Mockups

Three contrasting takes on the trivia display surface. All use the Spicy Broadcast color tokens; layout, typography, and personality differ. Click into each to see it full-screen.

01

Broadcast Studio

Literal TV game show. Three-zone layout — round branding ribbon, framed question card, dominant timer column. Most "produced" feel.

02

Question Spotlight

Minimal and theatrical. The question is the entire experience — enormous typography on near-black, thin meta in corners, almost no chrome. Quiet and confident.

03

Heat Cockpit

Lean into the spice metaphor. Vertical heat bar timer that drains, chunky team panel, ambient flame motifs. Most playful and visually characterful.
