MINIGAME_INTRO — Mockups

Three takes on the per-team briefing screen. All minimalist, each with a different "hero of the moment" choice.

01

Team Spotlight

Massive team name fills the screen. "You're up" eyebrow above, roster as small pills below, minigame chip in corner. Most distinct from ROUND_INTRO.

02

Lineup Card

Sports-roster intro. Team name at top, large vertical player list, minigame + sauce as bottom strip. Each player gets a moment.

03

Beat Reveal

Pairs with ROUND_INTRO. Animated cadence: "ON THE WINGS" → TEAM NAME → MINIGAME. Consistent design language across both intro phases.
