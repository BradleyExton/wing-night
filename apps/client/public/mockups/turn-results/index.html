TURN_RESULTS — Mockups

Three takes on the between-turn screen. Each tries a different emotional beat: closure, progress, or reward.

01

Team Done — Beat Reveal

Mirrors MINIGAME_INTRO cadence. Animated reveal of "TURN COMPLETE" → big faded team name → progress dots → next-step hint. Symmetry with the team-up screen.

02

Progress Dots

Minimal status. Big "TURN COMPLETE" header. Row of progress dots (filled for done, outlined for upcoming). Just-finished team highlighted. Fastest read.

03

Score Reveal

Show the points that team just earned. "+3 PTS · TEAM HEAT" with count-up. Most rewarding feedback. Requires extra prop wiring to pass pending points.
